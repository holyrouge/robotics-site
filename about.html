About Us

Who are we?

Stony Brook Robot Design Team is a multidisciplinary design team that is focused on providing aspiring engineers a collaborative learning environment where they can apply their skills to a variety of projects. The team's goal is to foster a passion for robotics, to build a community of dedicated engineers and to promote a culture of experiential learning at Stony Brook University.
