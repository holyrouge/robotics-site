What We Do

The Stony Brook Robotics Team is a multidisciplinary engineering design team that aspires to develop our members' technical skills through competition-based projects.

Competition-based Robotics Projects

Our members participate in annual competition-based robotics projects, challenging themselves to create the next generation of robots and experimenting with new technologies in mechanical design, fabrication, and automation.

Learn More

Technical Workshops + Mentorship

Every year, we host a series of technical workshops in everything from CAD to Python programming. In addition, we are focused on providing our members with long-term learning opportunities through our mentorship program.

Learn More

Robot Design Challenges

Every Fall semester, we host our annual Internal Competition, dedicated to providing roboticists and engineers all across Stony Brook University with the opportunity to learn new skills and to bring their ideas to fruition.

Learn More

Community Outreach

We are dedicated to supporting robotics and STEM learning both on and off campus through workshops, presentations, and mentoring. Supporting local communities and passionate roboticists is always our priority.
